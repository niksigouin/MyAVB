ABOUT US

OUR START

AVB Integration Services Inc. was incorporated in 2012. Prior to that we were known as 1080 Precision which was established in 2009. We recently moved our operations to a 1200 square foot office/warehouse located at 2285 St. Laurent Boulevard. This expansion allows us to better serve our client's needs in the Eastern Ontario and Ottawa market.

WE TAKE PRIDE IN EACH AND EVERY PROJECT

We really believe that no job is too small or too big for us to handle. We take pride in our work. We have the skills and expertise to handle anything from a single boardroom with a projector/screen to a complex long term multimillion dollar technology deployment. The AVB team keeps up to date with technology changes and we continue to improve our "tool box" with both manufacturing and industry training. We are fully insured and our staff and sub-contractors hold multiple security clearances with PWGSC.
