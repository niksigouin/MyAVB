Company Profile

AVB Integration Services Inc. is an Ottawa based company that provides Project Management, site supervision and technical installation skills to the Audio Visual and Broadcast industries. We specialize in the management, installation and integration of complex systems. Attention to detail, quality installation standards and executing with excellence are key to our success.

AVB is an owner operated company with a proven leader who has over 10 years experience and a commitment to customer service. Our staff is friendly, professional and knowledgeable, so you're in good hands.
